About Sattvic Soul Foundation

Our Journey Towards Sustainable Development

Who We Are

Sattvic Soul Foundation was established on September 10, 2019, with a vision to bring joy and positive change to underprivileged communities across India. Led by Mr. Satyabrata Samal and his dedicated associates, we have grown into a prominent organization operating across 13+ states and union territories.

Our Legal Status

Sattvic Soul Foundation is registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act of 2013 as a non-profit company. This legal framework ensures all our activities maintain the highest standards of transparency, with funds dedicated entirely to our social objectives rather than profit distribution.

Our Reach

Starting from grassroots work with underprivileged children in Odisha, we have grown to operate across 13+ states and union territories throughout India. Our programs reach both rural villages and urban slums, focusing on sustainable, community-driven development.

Our Focus Areas

Our work aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through four interconnected pillars:

  • Education (SDG 4) - Scholarships, school supplies, digital literacy, and infrastructure support for quality education access
  • Environment (SDGs 13, 15) - Tree plantation, waste management, water conservation, and climate action programs
  • Health (SDG 3) - Free medical camps, maternal care, nutrition programs, and sanitation awareness
  • Women Empowerment (SDG 5) - Vocational training, microfinance, entrepreneurship, and leadership development

Our Values

We believe in sustainable solutions that create lasting positive change. Our diverse membership is committed to addressing societal challenges through:

  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Community-Centric Approach
  • Sustainable Development
  • Inclusive Growth
  • Accountability
